Overview

The glory of India's northernmost regions can be witnessed on a visit to the cities of Delhi and Agra. They are home to some of our country's most prominent landmarks that lure tourists from far and wide all throughout the year. Explore the beautiful destinations of Delhi and Agra on your next vacation and get a chance to behold some of the most popular attractions of India such as the Taj Mahal, Qutub Minar, Agra Fort, Rajghat, India Gate, Lotus Temple, Jama Masjid and Red Fort, to name a few. Embark upon a 3-day tour with us and experience the best of these cities with friends and family.

Itinerary

Day 1 - Welcome to Delhi

On arrival in Delhi, our representative will pick you up from the airport/railway station/bus stand and transfer you to the hotel. Check in at the hotel, freshen up and gear up for a wonderful city exploration. Visit popular attractions like the Birla Temple, Jama Masjid (one of India's largest mosques), Rajghat (Mahatma Gandhiji's memorial), India Gate, President House, Parliament House, Humayun's Tomb, Lotus Temple and Qutub Minar. Birla Temple is one of the most famous and sacred temples in Delhi that exhibits a splendid architectural style. Worship the lord at the temple and proceed further to India Gate, a major landmark of India that was built as a war memorial to the brave soldiers of First World War. Qutub Minar is a marvellous structure and one of the world's tallest minarets to be made up of bricks. Also behold the beautiful architecture of Lotus Temple - a worship place of the Bahai designed by Fariborz Sahba, an Iranian architect. After an amazing city tour, you will return to the hotel for a pleasant stay.

Day 2 - Agra Sightseeing

Have a refreshing breakfast, check out from the hotel and meet your driver in the morning. Travel to Agra and check in at the hotel. Go on to visit the mesmerizing Taj Mahal, one of the 7 wonders of the world. Witness the breathtaking white-marble structure - Taj Mahal in all its glory, built by Emperor Shah Jahan for his beloved wife. Furthermore, visit the famous Agra Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site that stands as an architectural marvel of the Mughal style. Finally, return to your hotel for overnight stay.

Day 3 - More of Agra - End of Tour

After a good breakfast, check out from the hotel and go on to explore more of Agra on your own. You may opt to visit Wildlife SOS, Sheesh Mahal, Tomb of Itimad-ud-Daulah and Jahangir Mahal (leisure activities are not included in the package). Then drive to Delhi and proceed with your return travel.
